The Group

The Group

Quietly building, across sectors.

Headquartered in Mexico City, Isuria is a family-held investment house that owns and operates companies across energy, spirits, film and land. We are not a fund chasing a cycle — we are owners, present in the businesses we back, measuring returns in decades rather than quarters.

Each company in the group keeps its own name, its own craft and its own market. What they share is a standard: real assets, real operators, and a refusal to dilute quality for speed. We hold what we are proud to hold.

— Grupo Isuria
