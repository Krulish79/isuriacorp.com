About

Nº 01 — Ocean Raised Technology

SigriCo Aquaculture

Nº 05 — Agriculture

Coconut & Land

Our core agricultural project is coconut water — with room to grow into virgin coconut oil — produced in Papanoa, Guerrero. Through large family-owned plantations in the Zihuatanejo area, we have access to a minimum of 100,000 coconuts every month.
